【English Version】
I am not a puppet of the world. I was born with freedom flowing in my blood.
This is all the proof of how I rose from the ruins.

· Study Records: From a 242.5 score to daring to apply for STEP; from zero foundation to self-studying for AP exams. Every problem solved, every word memorized, every late night spent.
· Projects: The Chain of Becoming — applying "immutability" to my own growth. Psychological analyses — about family, relationships, and "why I am who I am".
· Essays & Reflections: Redemption fantasies, turning shields into signposts, moving from "I need to be seen" to "I am enough on my own".

I am not just applying to schools — I am showing how I became myself.

This path is my own. Not inherited, not given, built step by step by my own hands.

If you ask why I do all this —
All for the dreamland I crave so deeply, yet could not reach before.

If you ask if I’m afraid —
Yes. But after being afraid, I keep walking.

If you ask if it’s worth it —
Everything you’re reading here is the answer.
